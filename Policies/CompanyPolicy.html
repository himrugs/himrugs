Company Policy

Our Commitment to Excellence and Transparency

About HimRugs Company

HimRugs is a socially-conscious enterprise dedicated to preserving the rich heritage of Himalayan handcrafted rugs while empowering local artisans, particularly women. We operate with integrity, transparency, and a commitment to quality excellence.

🎯 Our Mission

To celebrate the artistry of traditional Himachali weaving, support women artisans, and bring authentic luxury rugs to discerning customers worldwide while maintaining the highest ethical standards.

✨ Our Vision

To become the world's most trusted brand for authentic, ethically-produced handcrafted rugs, creating sustainable livelihoods for artisan communities in Himachal Pradesh.

📋 Company Values

  • Authenticity: Every rug is genuinely handmade using traditional techniques
  • Quality: We maintain rigorous quality standards at every production stage
  • Sustainability: We use natural materials and eco-friendly practices
  • Social Responsibility: Fair wages and safe working conditions for all artisans
  • Transparency: Honest communication about pricing, processes, and materials
  • Customer-Centric: Your satisfaction is our ultimate goal

👥 Artisan Support Program

HimRugs is committed to supporting women artisans through:

  • Fair compensation above minimum wage standards
  • Safe and healthy working environments
  • Skill development and training programs
  • Social security and health benefits
  • Direct access to customers and market opportunities
  • Empowerment through financial independence

🌿 Environmental Commitment

We are committed to protecting the environment by:

  • Using 100% natural, biodegradable materials
  • Minimizing waste through efficient production
  • Supporting sustainable forestry practices
  • Reducing carbon footprint in shipping
  • Recycling and proper disposal of materials
