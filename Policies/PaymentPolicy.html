Payment Policy

Transparent Pricing and Secure Payment Processing

Clear & Transparent Payment Terms

HimRugs is committed to transparent pricing and secure payment processing. This policy outlines all payment terms, methods, and conditions to ensure a smooth transaction experience.

💳 Accepted Payment Methods

We accept multiple secure payment methods:

  • Credit/Debit Cards: Visa, MasterCard, American Express
  • Bank Transfer: Direct NEFT/RTGS transfers
  • UPI: Quick and secure digital payments
  • Digital Wallets: PayTM, Google Pay, Apple Pay
  • Cash on Delivery: Available in selected locations
  • Bank Checks: With proper verification

💰 Custom Order Payment Structure

For custom rug orders, we follow a three-stage payment process:

  • Initial Advance (30%): Due upon order confirmation to begin artisan work
  • Mid-Payment (40%): Once rug is 50% complete (photo provided for approval)
  • Final Payment (30%): Upon completion before shipping

This structure ensures quality work and protects both customer and artisan interests.

📋 Pricing & Quote Details

Our pricing includes:

  • Raw material costs (wool, dyes, natural fibers)
  • Artisan labor and craftsmanship
  • Quality control and inspection
  • Packaging and handling
  • GST and applicable taxes (as per government regulations)

Shipping costs are calculated separately based on location and weight.

🔒 Payment Security

Your payment security is our priority:

  • SSL encryption for all online transactions
  • PCI-DSS compliant payment gateway
  • Zero storage of sensitive card information
  • 256-bit encryption for data transmission
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Fraud detection and prevention measures

⏰ Payment Timeline

  • Order Confirmation: Within 2 hours of payment receipt
  • Work Commencement: Within 3-5 days after advance payment
  • Progress Photos: Shared within 15-20 days
  • Mid-Payment Due: Within 25-30 days of order
  • Completion Notification: Within project timeline (custom rug: 60-90 days)
  • Final Payment Request: 5 days before expected delivery

❌ Refund & Cancellation Policy

  • Before Work Start: Full refund within 7 days
  • After Work Start (0-30%): 80% refund minus material & labor costs
  • During Production (30-70%): 60% refund minus all costs incurred
  • Near Completion (70-100%): 30% refund (minimal refund due to completion stage)
  • Completed Orders: Only applicable under damage/defect clauses

Refunds are processed within 7-10 business days after approval.

🛡️ Dispute & Chargeback

In case of payment disputes:

  • Contact us within 7 days of the disputed transaction
  • Provide detailed explanation with transaction ID
  • We will investigate and respond within 5 business days
  • Documentation of payment and delivery will be provided
  • Unauthorized chargebacks may result in legal action

🎁 Discounts & Promotions

  • First 3 Orders: 65% discount (as advertised)
  • Bulk Orders: Custom discounts for 5+ rugs
  • Seasonal Sales: Special promotions during festivals
  • Referral Program: Discounts for customer referrals
  • Loyalty Rewards: Special pricing for repeat customers

Discounts cannot be combined unless explicitly stated. Terms & conditions apply.

💾 Invoice & Receipt

Upon payment, you will receive:

  • Official invoice with GST details
  • Payment confirmation and receipt
  • Order tracking number
  • Artisan and product details
  • Warranty certificate (where applicable)

⚠️ Failed Payment Handling

If your payment fails:

  • You will receive an immediate notification
  • Order processing is on hold until payment clears
  • Retry using alternative payment method
  • Contact support if issue persists
  • Order expires after 7 days of non-payment

🌍 International Payments

For international customers:

  • Payment in USD/EUR available upon request
  • Exchange rates as per current market rate
  • Additional international transaction fees may apply
  • Secure international payment gateway
  • Custom duty and taxes to be borne by customer
