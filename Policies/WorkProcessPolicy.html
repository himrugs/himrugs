Work Process Policy

From Design to Delivery: Our Complete Rug Making Journey

Handcrafted Excellence Through Traditional Methods

Every HimRugs masterpiece follows a meticulous, time-honored process. This policy details our complete work process, quality standards, and timelines to ensure transparency and customer satisfaction.

📋 Phase 1: Order Intake & Design (Days 1-5)

  • Order Confirmation: We acknowledge receipt within 2 hours
  • Design Consultation: Our design team contacts you via WhatsApp/Email
  • Design Finalization: Discuss colors, patterns, size, and materials
  • Custom Sketches: We create 2-3 design variations for your approval
  • Material Selection: Show wool samples and color swatches
  • Final Approval: Design lock-in before production begins
  • Quotes & Invoice: Detailed breakdown of costs sent for final confirmation

💼 Phase 2: Material Procurement (Days 3-8)

  • Wool Sourcing: Premium 100% natural wool from certified suppliers
  • Quality Check: Inspection for purity and fiber quality
  • Dyeing Process: Natural dyes using traditional Himalayan methods
  • Color Testing: Dye samples approved before full batch production
  • Drying & Storage: Proper conditioning of dyed wool (minimum 3-5 days)
  • Material Inventory: All materials organized and cataloged for artisan

🧵 Phase 3: Loom Preparation (Days 8-12)

  • Loom Selection: Choose appropriate traditional loom size for design
  • Base Threads Setup: Traditional warping with warp threads
  • Tension Adjustment: Precision calibration for uniform knot tension
  • Tool Preparation: Traditional tools (bobbins, combs, scissors) sterilized
  • Safety Check: All equipment inspected for artisan safety
  • Workspace Setup: Optimize ergonomics for comfort and quality

👩‍🎨 Phase 4: Weaving (Days 12-60 approximately)

This is where the magic happens!

  • Knot Work: Artisan hand-ties each knot using Persian hand-knotting technique
  • Pattern Precision: Following design pattern with 100% accuracy
  • Color Integration: Seamlessly blending colors as per design (no visible edges)
  • Daily Progress: 2-3 rows per day depending on complexity (approx. 150-200 knots/min)
  • Quality Control: Supervisor checks completed rows for defects during process
  • Photo Documentation: Progress photos every 25% completion
  • Artisan Breaks: Regular breaks to prevent strain and maintain quality

A medium-sized rug (6x4 feet) contains 350,000+ hand-tied knots!

📸 Progress Milestones & Communication

  • 25% Complete: Initial progress photo + video
  • 50% Complete: Detailed photo for mid-payment approval
  • 75% Complete: Extended progress documentation
  • 100% Complete: Final rug photos from multiple angles
  • Weekly Updates: Progress reports via WhatsApp (every Friday)
  • Custom Requests: Any design change requests honored (if feasible)

✂️ Phase 5: Finishing & Detailing (Days 60-65)

  • Loom Release: Careful cutting of rug from loom
  • Fringe Work: Hand-tying natural woolen fringe edges
  • Edge Binding: Traditional khandha (border) stitching
  • Trimming: Precise pile height trimming for uniformity
  • Shape Correction: Gently blocking to perfect shape
  • Detail Finishing: Hand-stitching any final details

🔍 Phase 6: Quality Inspection (Days 65-70)

  • 3-Point Inspection: Multiple quality checkpoints
  • Knot Verification: Random sampling of knot integrity (10,000+ knots checked)
  • Color Consistency: Uniform color distribution throughout rug
  • Structural Check: No loose knots, fraying, or defects
  • Dimensions: Verify exact size matches specifications (±2% tolerance)
  • Pattern Accuracy: Confirm design matches approved pattern
  • Final Photography: Professional photos for client approval

🧼 Phase 7: Cleaning & Treatment (Days 70-75)

  • Initial Wash: Gentle water wash to remove dust and excess dye
  • Conditioning: Natural wool conditioner application
  • Professional Dry Cleaning: Deep cleaning with specialized equipment
  • UV Fading Test: Ensure dyes are colorfast
  • Final Inspection: Last quality check post-cleaning
  • Conditioning Dry: Proper air-drying in controlled environment

📦 Phase 8: Packaging & Shipping (Days 75-80)

  • Protective Wrapping: Acid-free tissue paper and protective plastic
  • Quality Documentation: Certificate of authenticity included
  • Care Instructions: Detailed rug maintenance guide
  • Warranty Certificate: 1-year quality guarantee included
  • Secure Boxing: Standard shipping boxes with internal supports
  • Insurance: Full value insurance for transit protection
  • Tracking Setup: Real-time shipping tracking provided

🚚 Phase 9: Delivery & After-Sales (Days 80+)

  • Delivery Notification: 2 days before delivery via SMS/Email
  • Safe Delivery: Signature upon receipt with condition verification
  • Unboxing Support: WhatsApp guidance on proper unboxing
  • Installation Tips: Advice on rug placement and padding
  • 24-Hour Inspection: Customer inspection period for damage claims
  • Defect Claims: Report any issues within 48 hours
  • 1-Year Warranty: Free repair/replacement for manufacturing defects

⏱️ Standard Timeline Summary

  • Design Phase: 2-5 days
  • Material Prep: 3-8 days
  • Production: 45-75 days (depending on size & complexity)
  • Finishing: 5-10 days
  • Quality Check & Cleaning: 10-12 days
  • Packaging & Shipping: 3-7 days
  • Total Average Time: 60-90 days for custom orders

Rush orders available with 15-20% premium surcharge for 30-45 day delivery.

🎯 Quality Standards & Specifications

  • Material: 100% pure wool (no synthetic content)
  • Knot Density: 160-400 KPSI (Knots Per Square Inch) depending on design
  • Dyes: Natural plant-based dyes (no harmful chemicals)
  • Size Tolerance: ±2% from ordered dimensions
  • Color Variance: ±5% acceptable natural variation
  • Defect Rate: Less than 0.1% (virtually defect-free)
  • Durability: 50+ years with proper care

🔧 Revisions & Modifications

  • Design Changes (Before Work Starts): Free, unlimited modifications
  • Minor Changes (0-25% Complete): Free up to 2 changes
  • Mid-Work Changes (25-50%): May incur additional cost (10-20% extra)
  • Late Changes (50%+): Limited modifications; consult management
  • Pattern Adjustments: May require timeline extension

❌ Quality Issues & Solutions

If issues are found during quality checks:

  • Minor Defects: Fixed within 3-5 days without additional cost
  • Major Defects: Complete redo by artisan (on HimRugs' cost)
  • Defect Reporting: Report within 48 hours of delivery
  • Resolution Timeline: Full resolution within 15 days
  • Replacement: Free replacement if defect claim valid

🌟 Our Artisan Community

Every rug helps support local women artisans:

  • Fair wage compensation (above government minimum)
  • Safe, clean working environments
  • Health and social security benefits
  • Skill development and training programs
  • Each artisan receives recognition in your rug's certificate
