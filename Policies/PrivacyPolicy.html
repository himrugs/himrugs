Privacy Policy

Your Trust and Data Protection Matter to Us

Data Protection & Privacy

At HimRugs, we are committed to protecting your personal information and respecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when visiting our website and using our services.

📱 Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, postal address
  • Purchase Information: Order details, payment method, billing address
  • Communication Data: Messages, inquiries, and feedback you send us
  • Usage Data: Browser type, IP address, pages visited, time spent on site
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, device identifiers
  • Cookies: Tracking information for website functionality and improvement

🎯 How We Use Your Information

Your information is used for:

  • Processing and fulfilling your orders
  • Sending order confirmations and updates
  • Responding to your inquiries and customer support
  • Improving our website, products, and services
  • Sending promotional offers (with your consent)
  • Fraud prevention and security measures
  • Complying with legal obligations

🔒 Data Security

We implement the following security measures:

  • SSL encryption for all transactions
  • Secure payment gateway integration
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limited access to personal information
  • Employee confidentiality agreements
  • Secure data storage and backup systems
  • Monitoring for unauthorized access attempts

👥 Third-Party Information Sharing

We do NOT sell, trade, or rent your personal information. However, we may share information with:

  • Service Providers: Payment processors, shipping partners, email services
  • Legal Requirements: Law enforcement when required by law
  • Business Partners: Only with your explicit consent
  • Analytics Services: To improve website performance (anonymized data)

🍪 Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses cookies to:

  • Remember your preferences and login information
  • Analyze website traffic and user behavior
  • Provide personalized content and recommendations
  • Enable social media features

You can control cookie settings in your browser preferences.

✉️ Email Communications

If you opt-in to our mailing list, we will send you promotional emails about new products, updates, and special offers. You can unsubscribe at any time by clicking the "Unsubscribe" link in our emails.

🔐 Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data (Right to be Forgotten)
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Request a copy of your data

👶 Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware of such collection, we will delete the information immediately.

🌍 International Data Transfers

If your data is transferred internationally, we ensure it is protected with adequate safeguards and complies with applicable data protection laws.
