Our Story

Preserving the Soul of Himachal, One Knot at a Time.

A Legacy of Craftsmanship

HimRugs was born from a deep-rooted passion for the traditional weaving arts of the Himalayas. In the serene valleys of Himachal Pradesh, weaving is not just a skill; it's a cultural heartbeat. Our rugs are crafted using age-old techniques that have been preserved by local communities for centuries.

We believe in the beauty of imperfection and the soul that only human hands can provide. Every rug we create is a unique piece of art, reflecting the rugged beauty of the mountains and the warmth of the people who call them home.

Empowering Women Artisans

The Hands Behind the Magic

Skill & Dedication

Our team consists of highly skilled women from local villages who have mastered the art of hand-knotting. By providing fair wages and a supportive environment, we empower them to sustain their families while keeping their heritage alive.

Sustainable Living

We focus on sustainable practices, using locally sourced wool and natural dyes whenever possible. Our goal is to create a positive impact on both the environment and the community.
